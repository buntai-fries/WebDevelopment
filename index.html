My Favourite Movies of All Time:

These are list of my favourite movie that I watched so far.

1. The God father

The Godfather (1972) is famous for revolutionizing cinema through its masterful blend of Shakespearian-level drama,
atmospheric storytelling, and unforgettable performances, particularly by Marlon Brando and Al Pacino.
Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, it transformed a pulp crime novel into a serious, artistic exploration of family, loyalty,
capitalism, and corruption, making it a universally acclaimed masterpiece of American cinema.

2. Fight Club

Unhappy with his capitalistic lifestyle, a white-collared insomniac forms an underground fight club with Tyler, a careless
soap salesman. Soon, their venture spirals down into something sinister.

3. I want to eat your pancrease.

One day, "Me" - a highschooler - found a paperback in the hospital. The "Disease Coexistence Journal" was its title. It was a diary
that "Me"'s classmate, Sakura Yamauchi, had written in secret. Inside, it was written that due to her pancreatic disease,
her days were numbered. And thus, "Me" coincidentally went from Just-a-Classmate to a Secret-Knowing-Classmate. It was as if he were
being drawn to her, who was his polar opposite. However, the world presented the girl that was already suffering from an illness with
an equally cruel reality.
